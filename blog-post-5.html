The Art of Data Visualization: Making Sense of Big Data

Turning complex datasets into compelling, actionable visuals.

June 28, 2025 • by Alex Chen • Category: Data Analytics

Choosing the Right Chart

Bar charts for comparison, line charts for trends, scatter plots for relationships—match your chart type to the insight you want to convey.

Design Principles

Prioritize clarity and readability: use consistent scales, limit colors, and highlight only what matters.

Interactivity

Interactive dashboards enable users to explore data, drill down into segments, and discover patterns that static charts might hide.
